Her Story

Built on a Belief That Everyone Deserves a Home

Britnae didn't get into real estate for the commissions — she got in because she saw too many people in her community give up on homeownership before they ever got started. Told they needed more money, better credit, more time. She knew that wasn't the full story.

So she became a licensed REALTOR® in Texas, joined Provision Realty, and specialized in exactly the programs most agents overlook: new construction with low deposit requirements, first-time buyer grants, and builder incentives that make owning a home in DFW or Houston more attainable than people realize.

Today, she serves clients across Dallas-Fort Worth and Houston — and with over 72,000 people following her journey on Instagram, she's become one of the most trusted real estate voices in Texas.

Common Questions

You Asked, She Answers

Do I need perfect credit to buy a home? +
Not at all. Many first-time buyer programs work with credit scores starting at 580 or even lower. During your free Action Plan call, Britnae will review your full financial picture and identify which programs you qualify for right now.
How much money do I actually need to start? +
Less than you think. Several new construction communities in DFW and Houston require as little as $2,500–$5,000 to reserve your lot and start building. Britnae can walk you through exactly what's needed at each stage.
What's the difference between new construction and resale? +
New construction means you're buying a brand-new home — often with the ability to customize finishes, choose your lot, and benefit from builder warranties. Resale homes are previously owned. Each has pros and cons, and Britnae will help you decide which is right for your situation.
Is Britnae's service free for buyers? +
Yes! As a buyer, you typically don't pay your agent's commission — the seller or builder does. Britnae's representation costs you nothing out of pocket in most transactions.
Can I buy a home if I'm relocating from another state? +
Absolutely. Britnae regularly works with out-of-state buyers relocating to DFW and Houston. She offers virtual tours, remote contract management, and can help you close before you even arrive in Texas.
How long does the homebuying process take? +
It varies. A move-in ready resale home can close in 30–45 days once you're under contract. New construction timelines depend on the build stage — some communities have quick move-in homes ready in weeks, others take 6–12 months to build. Britnae will set clear expectations from day one.
